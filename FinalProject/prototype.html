Our product is an a special type of essential oil diffuser. Essential Oil Diffusion is the process of dispersing essential oils so that their aroma fills a room or an area with the natural fragrance. Our idea is to blend a specific number of oils and you could diffuse it in your house. This model keeps the mosquitoes away. Our other model does the opposite it attracts the mosquitoes to the area and traps them. Mosquitoes: Citronella, peppermint, lemon, eucalyptus, basil, clove, thyme, lemongrass, geranium, and lavender.
